All about business

Business is one of the fundamental streams of career for people all over the world. In 2020, it was estimated that there were approximately 213.65 million companies all around the world. This shows the impact business has on all countries, and the importance it holds around the globe. There is no specific definition when it comes to business, but loosely it is the ‘organized effort of individuals to produce and sell, for a profit, the goods and services, that satisfy society's needs’.

Starting a business is indeed not everyone's cup of tea. It requires a great deal of planning and preparation, under which comes the assembling of a team, assignment of roles, choosing of industries, brainstorming and launching a product, and much more. At the end of the day, business is not all about being successful and making a profit off of a product or service, it is about the journey of self-discovery one goes through to leave a mark in the world of successful tycoons. Five such businessmen also embarked on this journey, and to this day, whether living or not, continue to have a huge impact in the business world. These five individuals could not have imagined their what value their success would hold today, not only for themselves but for the world. Their innovative ideas and products paved the path for the world's population in many ways, whether that be electric cars or wireless connections with strangers.

These five businessmen are Bill Gates, Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, Steve Jobs, and Mark Zuckerberg.
